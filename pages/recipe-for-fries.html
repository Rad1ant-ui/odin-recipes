Fries recipe

Description

Easy to make fries, delicious and skinny cut for your pleassure

    Ingredients
  • 10 potatoes
  • Salt and pebber
  • vinegar
  • oil
      • Directions

  • cut the 10 potatoes in skinny pieces
  • add salt and pebber
  • add oil
  • add vinegar
